    Claremont Munchies is an entirely student-run organization delivering your favorite late night goods. We are simple people — when we're hungry, we like some good munchies.

    We also think that other people, both on our campus and around the world, would like some good munchies too. So, 25% of our profits are given to The Hunger Project, an organization working to alleviate the pains of world hunger. So, get a burger, give a burger. Cheers.
